Brigadeiro

Description

There is a great Brazilian sweet that is like a bonbon and extremely scrumptious and delicious called brigadeiro! This is a great type of sweet that is loved here in Brazil!

Try it! You can make it in the microwave too, just remember to stir every minute.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa

  • 1 tablespoon butter

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine cocoa, butter and condensed milk.

    Cook, stirring, until thickened, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let rest until cool enough to handle.

    Form into small balls and eat at once or chill until serving.
